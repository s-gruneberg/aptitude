Math Section Strategies

Tips and strategies to help you succeed on the math portion of the Electrical aptitude test.

Overview

The math section of the Electrical aptitude test covers several key areas:

  • Algebra: Solving equations, working with variables, and manipulating expressions
  • Functions: Understanding function notation, graphs, and relationships
  • Number Series: Identifying patterns and sequences
  • Mathematical Reasoning: Problem-solving and logical thinking
General Test-Taking Strategies
  • Read each question carefully and identify what is being asked
  • Show your work to avoid calculation errors
  • Manage your time effectively - don't spend too long on any single question
  • If you're stuck, eliminate obviously wrong answers first
  • Review your answers if time permits
