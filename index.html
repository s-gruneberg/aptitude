What to Expect

An electrical apprenticeship aptitude test consists of two main sections:

  • Math Section: 45 minutes of algebra, functions, number series, and mathematical reasoning
  • Reading Comprehension: 50 minutes of understanding written passages and answering questions
  • Additional Topics: Depending on the test, you may also be tested on other topics such as:
    • Mechanical Aptitude
    • Spatial Reasoning (paper folding)
    • Other topics may vary by test

These practice exams are designed to help you familiarize yourself with the math and readingquestion types and format you'll encounter on test day. Additional topics may be tested on the test day, so be sure to practice those as well.

How to Use This Site

  1. Start with a full practice exam: Take Exam 1 and mimic the test environment. Set a timer for 45 minutes for the math portion, then take a 10-minute break, followed by 50 minutes for the reading portion.
  2. Review and study: After completing the exam, review the explanations and study the strategies on our Math Strategies and Reading Strategies pages.
  3. Practice sections separately: Use the Math Practice and Reading Practice pages to focus on specific areas where you need improvement.
  4. Take the second exam: Complete Exam 2 to measure your progress and further prepare for test day.
  5. Get extra practice and explanations: Use an LLM like Chat GPT or Claude to get extra practice questions and explanations.
