About This Site

A free educational resource to help you prepare for an electricalapprenticeship aptitude test.

Our Mission

This website was created to provide free, accessible practice resources for individuals preparing for an electrical apprenticeship aptitude test. We believe that everyone should have access to quality test preparation materials without financial barriers.

Our goal is to help you feel confident and prepared when you take the actual test by providing practice questions, and study strategies.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is this site affiliated with IBEW?

No, this is an independent educational resource. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers or any official testing organization.

Is this site really free?

Yes! All practice materials and resources on this site are completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or paywalls.

What does a typical electrical apprenticeship aptitude test cover?

The aptitude test typically includes two main sections: mathematics (algebra, functions, number series) and reading comprehension. Our practice materials are designed to help you prepare for these areas. Some tests cover additional tpoics

How should I use this site to prepare?

We recommend starting with a full practice exam and mimicking the test environment. To do so, set a timer for 45 minutes for the math portion, and 50 minutes for the reading portion. Take a 10 minute break between the two sections. After completing the full practice exam, review the explanations and study the strategies. Then, practice the math and reading sections separately. Finally, take the additional full practice exam to see your progress.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! This site is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Since the test is taken on a computer, that's the best way to practice.
