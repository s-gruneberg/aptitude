Reading Comprehension Strategies

Tips and strategies to help you succeed on the reading comprehension portion of the Electrical aptitude test.

Overview

The reading comprehension section tests your ability to:

  • Understand and analyze written passages
  • Identify main ideas and supporting details
  • Make inferences and draw conclusions
  • Understand vocabulary in context
  • Recognize author's purpose and tone
General Reading Strategies
  • Read the passage carefully before looking at the questions
  • Identify the main idea and key points as you read
  • Pay attention to topic sentences and concluding statements
  • Note any transition words that indicate relationships between ideas
  • Don't rely on prior knowledge - base answers only on the passage
Question Types

You'll encounter several types of questions:

  • Main Idea: Identify the central theme or primary purpose
  • Detail Questions: Find specific information stated in the passage
  • Inference Questions: Draw conclusions based on implied information
  • Vocabulary: Determine word meaning from context
  • Author's Purpose: Understand why the author wrote the passage

Detailed strategies for each question type will be added in future updates.

Time Management
  • Allocate time to read the passage thoroughly
  • Answer questions you're confident about first
  • Return to difficult questions if time permits
  • Don't spend too much time on any single question
  • Review your answers if you have time remaining
Common Pitfalls to Avoid
  • Don't choose answers based on outside knowledge
  • Avoid answers that are too extreme or absolute
  • Watch out for answer choices that are partially correct but not the best answer
  • Don't rush - make sure you understand what each question is asking
  • Be careful with questions that ask what the passage does NOT say
